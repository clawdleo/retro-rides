The Hunt for Vintage Steel

Every bike here is hand-picked from across Europe. I search daily for forgotten classics — Stumpjumpers tucked in Austrian garages, Zaskars gathering dust in Italian sheds.

When I find one, I bring it back. New cables, fresh grease, tuned derailleurs. Some need full restoration. Others just need someone who appreciates what they are.

These aren't just bikes. They're the machines that defined mountain biking.
