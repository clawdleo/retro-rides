The Hunt for Vintage Steel

Every bike in my collection is hand-picked from across Europe. I search marketplaces daily, looking for hidden gems — classic Stumpjumpers, rare Kleins, forgotten GT Zaskars.

When I find one, I bring it back to life. New cables, fresh grease, tuned derailleurs. Some get full restorations. Others just need a little care.

These aren't just bikes — they're pieces of mountain biking history, built when frames were steel and rides were real.
