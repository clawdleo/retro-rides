## Content Themes by Month

### Month 1: Establish Presence
- Week 1: Introduction posts ("Who is Pat Ina")
- Week 2: First bike feature (if available) or education content
- Week 3: "The Hunt" series begins
- Week 4: Brand history (Klein, Yeti, etc.)

### Month 2: Build Community
- Engage with other vintage MTB accounts
- Feature follower bikes
- Start "What's it worth?" posts
- First reel/video content

### Month 3: Establish Authority
- Buyer's guide series
- Price trend analysis
- Collaboration with other collectors
- First sale through social

## One Bike = 10+ Posts

When you get a new bike, extract maximum content:

1. **Pickup Story** (X) - "Just picked up this beauty in Graz"
2. **First Look** (IG) - Dirty, as-found condition
3. **Detail Shots** (IG) - Decals, patina, components (3-5 posts)
4. **Cleaning Process** (IG Reel) - Before/during/after
5. **Component Spotlight** (IG) - "This XTR groupset..."
6. **History Post** (IG) - "The 1993 Zaskar was..."
7. **Hero Shot** (IG) - Final glamour photo
8. **For Sale Announcement** (Both) - Price, specs, link
9. **Sold Celebration** (Both) - "Off to its new home in Munich"
